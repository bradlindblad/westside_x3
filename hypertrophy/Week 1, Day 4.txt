Week 1—Day 4 of the Westside Barbell Conjugate Hypertrophy Program
Zercher Squat—Speed Work
Complete 5 sets of 5 reps with 30 seconds of rest. Focus on performing clean, fast reps with solid technique under tension.
Deadlift
Complete 5 sets of 15–20 repetitions. This is your volume pull for the week—own your positions and rest adequately to maintain high intensity.
Zercher Split Squats
Perform 5 sets of 10 to 12 reps for each leg. Control each repetition. No bouncing and no shortcuts.
Zercher Side Bends
Complete 3 sets of 10 repetitions on each side. Stay tall and bend smoothly against the resistance.
